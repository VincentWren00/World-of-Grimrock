Welcome to Cithrel

The country of Cithrel has a long and rich history of friendship and culture. A small nation of the south east coast of Grimrock. This nation is mainly inhabited by humans and elves.

The country of Cithrel is respected and admired by the other races. It was a haven of peace, wisdom, handcrafted goods and shared its knowledge and trades with the lands of Wimrith, Hulis, Grison, Elyes, and Ulmug.

The country of Cithrel are ruled by a Leaders. The most famous of these were Elarion, a half human and half elf, who helped the country in overseas trading and produced great wealth. And with this they have formed good connections and trades from the other nations.

The reign of Elarion marked their wisdom and generosity. The country was revered by the world as a great connection to have in goods. It became one of the top nations to trade with and seek guidance and support. The country was inhabited by loyal and enlightened people.

Today, Cithrel is still a bright and noble land. The people who live there are honorable and compassionate. They are constantly helping the world, and have much hope for the future. They cherish their divine heritage and values.
