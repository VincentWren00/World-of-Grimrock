Welcome to Amer

The land of Amer has a long and grim history of struggle and survival. Amer is a unclaimed country due to lack of interest due to it having a wrap of being the refuge place for the bad and criminal.

Amer's land geography has been known as rugged and harsh land. The country of Amer was feared and heeded by the other nations. It was home to thieves and outlaws, and endured hardships and dangers.

From hearsay of the elders from nearby nations, the land was once populated by many races and cultures, who fought and lived together. But due to the harshness of the land it just became home to the cruel and harsh.

Today, Amer is still talked about and is unclaimed.
