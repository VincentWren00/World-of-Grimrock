Welcome to the World of Grimrock

Grimrock is a world of eight countries with their own nations, cultures and more. Grimrock has at least two kingdoms, three communities, an mysterious abandoned island, two unclaimed countries, and a trail of mountains. There's the kingdom of Grinson and Wimrith. There are the communities of Ulmug, Hulis and Cithrel. The lost and mysterious island called Mistore. Two unclaimed lands of Amer and Elyes. Finally, the mountain trail known as Wilval Peaks. These places are what make Grimrock what it is today, a world full of lots of diverse lives and cultures.
